Biography

I am joining the Westlake University as a Ph.D student, advised by Yue Zhang. Currently, I am working at Tianjin University in Tianjin, China under the supervision of Meishang Zhang. I obtained my Master of Science (M.Sc.) degree from CQUPT in 2018, advised by Professor Jian-Xun Mi.

My primary research interests lie in natural language processing, computer vision and machine learning. I am particularly interested in applying deep learning techniques for natural language understanding, neural machine translation and graph representations.
